# 📝 Prompts para Geração de Imagens de UI - LaCuisine

Este documento contém os prompts otimizados em inglês para gerar as telas do sistema LaCuisine em ferramentas de Inteligência Artificial como Midjourney, DALL-E 3, ou Leonardo.ai.

O estilo principal estabelecido inclui uma paleta luxuosa (palha, marrom intenso espresso e dourado rico) e tipografia elegante e serifada.

## Tela 1: Login / Autenticação (A Porta de Entrada)
**Prompt:**
```text
UI/UX design of a luxury recipe SaaS application, login screen. Split screen layout. On the left: a high-end, moody food photography of a gourmet dish with warm lighting. On the right: an elegant minimalistic login form on a pale straw background (#FAFAF9). Deep espresso brown (#1C1917) serif typography "LaCuisine" at the top. A subtle, solid rich gold (#CA8A04) "Sign In" button. Clean, large margins, sophisticated white space, premium restaurant vibe, Dribbble style, high resolution, 8k --ar 16:9
```

## Tela 2: Dashboard Insight (O Painel do Chef)
**Prompt:**
```text
UI/UX design of a premium culinary web application dashboard. Clean grid-based layout. Pale straw background (#FEF3C7). Elegant serif typography for section headers in deep espresso brown. Sans-serif for data and numbers. Highlights and thin 1px dividers in rich golden color. Top section: minimal summary cards with large thin numbers and subtle shadows. Primary button: "AI Generate Recipe" with a glowing gold outline. Professional, sophisticated Michelin-star restaurant management feel, editorial layout, plenty of white space, UI/UX masterpiece, 8k --ar 16:9
```

## Tela 3: O Estúdio de Criação / Gerador de IA (Task-Driven)
**Prompt:**
```text
UI/UX design of an AI recipe generator interface for a luxury SaaS app. Vintage recipe book meets modern minimal web design. Pale straw background layout. A large, prominent search bar in the center resembling a modern typewriter input with the text "Describe your dish...", accompanied by a golden star icon. Below the bar, a clean list of generated ingredients fading in. Features elegant classic serif typography for titles, and highly legible sans-serif for the ingredients list. Deep espresso brown text. Rich gold accents. Extremely clean, uncluttered, elegant, high contrast UI, 8k --ar 16:9
```

## Tela 4: Ficha Técnica Operacional (O Coração do SaaS)
**Prompt:**
```text
UI/UX design of a technical culinary recipe sheet (Ficha Técnica) for a luxury restaurant management app. Data-heavy but extremely elegant table layout. Pale straw background. The table header is a dark espresso brown with pale straw text. Very thin, elegant golden horizontal lines separating the ingredient rows. Large elegant serif typography for the recipe title "Truffle Risotto" at the top left. Monospaced or clean sans-serif fonts for the weights and costs columns. Minimalist, high readability, editorial data visualization, sophisticated, 8k --ar 16:9
```

## Tela 5: Grid de Receitas (A "Revista" do Chef)
**Prompt:**
```text
UI/UX design of a recipe library screen for a luxury culinary app. Gallery view resembling a high-end food magazine editorial. Pale straw background. Grid of vertical recipe cards featuring flawless, mouth-watering gourmet food photography. Below each image: a classic serif title in dark brown and a tiny, minimalist category tag. No visible buttons or clutter, just the images and typography. Sophisticated, minimalist, premium editorial aesthetic, Dribbble style, UI/UX masterpiece, 8k --ar 16:9
```

---

### Dicas de Uso nas Ferramentas (Ex: Midjourney)
- `--ar 16:9` foi adicionado ao final para garantir a proporção widescreen, ideal para desktop.
- Para obter formatos móveis (mobile), altere para `--ar 9:16` e adicione o termo `mobile app interface`.
- As cores exatas em HEX foram adicionadas como guias descritivas, pois as IAs entendem muito bem seus nomes descritivos ("Pale straw", "Rich gold").
